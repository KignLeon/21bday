Discipline

Quick Rules

💱
Always pay in pesos. Paying in USD at vendors costs you 3–8% extra on every transaction.
🏧
BBVA or Santander ATMs only in Tulum Pueblo. Tourist ATMs near the beach charge predatory fees.
🗺️
Google Maps only. Host specifically said Apple Maps and Waze will send you to the wrong location.
🌿
Reef-safe sunscreen at cenotes. Non-reef-safe will get you turned away at the entrance.
🪪
Leave passport at condo. Carry a photo of it on excursion days — less risk if bag goes missing.
🥃
Beach clubs: one real night max. Easy to blow $300+ per night. Keep it to your birthday.
💵
Bring cash pesos to cenotes. Most are cash-only. Withdraw before you leave Pueblo in the morning.
🚗
Topes (speed bumps) everywhere. Slow down — they will destroy a car at even moderate speed.
