⚡ Quick Rules
Always pay in pesos. Paying in USD at vendors costs you 3–8% extra every time.
BBVA or Santander ATMs only in Tulum town. Tourist ATMs near the beach charge predatory fees.
Google Maps only for navigation. The host specifically said Apple Maps and Waze will mislead you.
Reef-safe sunscreen at cenotes. They check. Non-reef-safe will get you turned away.
Leave passport at condo. Just carry a photo of it on excursion days. Less risk if bag gets lost.
Beach clubs: one real night max. It's easy to blow $300+ per night there. Keep it to your birthday.
Bring cash pesos to cenotes. Most are cash-only. Withdraw before you leave town in the morning.
Topes (speed bumps). Everywhere in Tulum. Slow down — they will destroy a car at full speed.
